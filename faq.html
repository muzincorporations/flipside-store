FAQ

How can we help you today?

How do I receive my digital product?
Digital keys and download links are sent instantly to your provided email address once payment has been confirmed by our staff or system.
What payment methods do you accept?
We currently support PayPal (Friends & Family) for instant verification, alongside Bitcoin and Ethereum for crypto enthusiasts. All payments are processed through secure, encrypted gateways.
Can I get a refund if I change my mind?
No. Due to the high-value nature of digital licenses and the logistics of our dropshipping chain, all sales are final. We encourage you to review our Return Policy for exceptional cases involving invalid keys.
Is there a warranty on physical products?
Yes! Most of our premium physical electronics come with a 12-month manufacturer warranty. Please check the specific product description for warranty details.
How long does shipping take?
For UK physical products, delivery typically takes 2-3 business days. You will receive a tracking number as soon as your order has been dispatched.
How do I become a reseller?
Simply head over to our "Become a Reseller" page, fill out the registration form, and you'll receive your unique referral link to start generating commissions immediately!
