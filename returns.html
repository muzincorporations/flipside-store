⚠️ STRICT NO-RETURN POLICY

At FlipSide, all sales are final. Due to the nature of our products (digital assets and dropshipped goods), we do not offer returns, refunds, or exchanges under any circumstances.

1. Digital Products

All digital products, including but not limited to activation keys, software licenses, and downloadable content, are delivered instantly or upon payment verification. Once a digital key or link has been revealed or sent to your email, it is considered "used" and cannot be returned or refunded.

2. Physical Products

Our physical products are fulfilled through specialized dropshipping partners. To keep our prices competitive and maintain our logistics chain, we cannot accept returns for physical items. Please ensure you select the correct size, color, or model before completing your purchase.

3. Exceptional Circumstances

If you receive a digital key that is verified as "invalid" or "already used" **at the time of delivery**, please contact our support team within 24 hours. We will investigate the issue and, if verified, provide a replacement. This does not apply to keys that are banned or disabled due to user activity after successful activation.

4. Order Cancellations

Orders can only be cancelled if they have not yet been "Processed" or "Paid" in our system. Once an order is marked as Paid (manually or automatically), it enters our fulfillment queue and cannot be cancelled.
