Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you make a purchase, register as a reseller, or communicate with us. This includes:

  • Name and email address
  • Shipping address (for physical items)
  • Phone number
  • Payment information (processed securely by 3rd parties)
  • Referral codes used

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Manage reseller accounts and commissions
  • Send transaction emails and updates
  • Improve our platform and security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage & Security

We use Google Firebase for hosting and database services. Your data is stored securely in Firebase's data centers. While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no system is 100% secure.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with service providers needed to operate our business (e.g., Firebase, EmailJS, PayPal, and our dropshipping fulfillment partners).

5. Cookies

We use local storage and cookies to remember your shopping cart and reseller referral status. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features of the platform may not function correctly.
