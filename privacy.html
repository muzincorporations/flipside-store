Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you make a purchase, register as a reseller, or communicate with us. This includes:

  • Full Name and secure email identity
  • Shipping address (exclusively for physical logistics)
  • Contact phone number
  • Referral codes for commission attribution

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to facilitate the high-end service we provide:

  • Fulfillment of digital and physical orders
  • Accurate commission tracking for resellers
  • Transaction alerts and platform security
  • Compliance with global digital trade regulations

3. Security & Integrity

FlipSide utilizes Google Cloud and Firebase architectures to host our data with enterprise-grade encryption. We continuously monitor our systems to protect your business and personal information from unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Integration

We do not monetize your personal data through sales to third parties. Information is shared only with verified service providers necessary for your transaction, such as payment gateways, email delivery systems, and logistics partners.
